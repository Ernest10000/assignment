These are the steps i would take to Develop a Website

1.Define their goals and objectives: Clarify the purpose of the website, identify the target audience and determine what you want to achieve with it.

2.Plan the content and structure: Create an outline that outlines the main sections and pages you need on your website. Consider user experience (UX) principles for easy navigation.

3.Select a unique and memorable domain name that reflects your brand or business.

4.Design the layout and visuals: Decide on the overall look and feel of your website's design, including color scheme, typography, imagery, logo placement, etc.

4.Create webpages: Start building individual webpages based on planned content hierarchy using appropriate technologies (HTML/CSS). Ensure consistency in design elements across all pages for cohesive branding.

5.Test & Launch : Conduct thorough testing and debugging across multiple browsers to ensuring proper functionality & validate all links,images,forms. Once satisfied with results then launch the website

6.Monitor & Maintain : Regularly update content and security. To keep your website secure & running smoothly.
